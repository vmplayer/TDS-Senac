# Atividade 002 - Inglês

Afirmação -> Contração <-> Tradução <-> Negação -> Interrogação

1. My brother is tired. -> My brother's tired. <-> Meu irmão está cansado. <-> My brother isn't tired. <- Is my brother tired?

2. They are at school. -> They're at school. <-> Eles estão na escola. <-> They are not at work. <- Are they at school?

3. I am very happy today. -> I'm very happy today. <-> Eu estou muito feliz hoje. <-> I'm not happy today. <- Am I very happy today?

4. My dog is sleeping. -> My dog's sleeping. <-> Meu cachorro está dormindo. <-> My dog isn't sleeping. <- Is my dog sleeping?

5. We are ready for the test -> We're ready fot the test. <-> Nós estamos prontos para a prova. <->  We aren't ready for the test. <- Are we ready for the test?

6. You are my best friend. -> You're my best friend. <-> Você é o meu melhor amigo. <-> You aren't my best friend. <- Are you my best friend?

7. The children are playing outside. -> The children're playing outside. <-> As crianças estão brincando lá fora. <-> The children are not playing outside. <- Are the children playing on the outside?

8. I am from Brazil. -> I'm from Brazil. <-> Eu sou do Brasil. <-> I am not from Brazil. <- Am I from Brazil?

9. She is a doctor. -> She's a doctor. <-> Ela é uma doutora. <-> She isn't a doctor. <- Is she a doctor?

10. My cat is black. -> My cat's black. <-> Meu gato é preto. <-> My cat is not black. <- Is my cat black?

11. My mother is in the kitchen. -> My mother's on the kitchen. <-> Minha mãe está na cozinha. <-> My mother isn't in the kitchen. <- Is my mother at the kitchen?

12. The teachers are in the classroom. -> The teachers're in the classroom. <-> Os professores estão na sala de aula. <-> The teachers are not in the classroom. <- Are the teachers in the classroom?

13. He is at home now. -> He's at home now. <-> Ele está em casa agora. <-> He isn't at home now. <- Is he at home now?

14. He is a good student -> He's a good student. <-> Ele é um bom aluno. <-> He is not a good student. <- Is he a good student?

15. It is a beautiful day. -> It's a beautiful day. <-> Hoje está um dia lindo. <-> It isn't a beautiful day. <- Is it a beautiful day?

16. My friends and I are excited. -> My friends and I are excited. <-> Meus amigos e eu estamos animados. <-> My friends and I are not excited. <- Are my friends and I excited?

17. The book is on the table. -> The book's on the table. <-> O livro está na mesa. <-> The book isn't on the table. <- Is the book on the table?

18. I am hungry. -> I'm hungry. <-> Eu estou com fome. <-> I am not hungry. <- Am I hungry?

19. You are very kind. -> You're very kind. <-> Você é muito gentil. <-> You are not very kind. <- Are you very kind?

20. My parents are at work. -> My parents're at work. <-> Meus pais estão no trabalho. <-> My parents aren't at work. <- Are my parents at work?
