# Atividade 002 - Inglês

1. My brother is tired. -> Meu irmão está cansado. <-> My brother isn't tired. <- Is my brother tired?

2. They are at school. -> Eles estão na escola. <-> They are not at work. <- Are they at school?

3. I am very happy today. -> Eu estou muito feliz hoje. <-> I'm not happy today. <- Am I very happy today?

4. My dog is sleeping. -> Meu cachorro está dormindo. <-> My dog isn't sleeping well. <- Is my dog sleeping well?

5. We are ready for the test -> Nós estamos prontos para a prova. <->  We aren't ready for the test. <- Are we ready for the test?

6. You are my best friend. -> Você é o meu melhor amigo. <-> You aren't my best friend. <- Are you my best friend?

7. The children are playing outside. -> As crianças estão brincando lá fora. <-> The children are not playing outside. <- Are the children playing on the outside?

8. I am from Brazil. -> Eu sou do Brasil. <-> I am not from Brazil. <- Am I from Brazil?

9. She is a doctor. -> Ela é uma doutora. <-> She isn't a doctor. <- Is she a doctor?

10. My cat is black. -> Meu gato é preto. <-> My cat is not black. <- Is my cat black?

11. My mother is in the kitchen. -> Minha mãe está na cozinha. <-> My mother isn't in the kitchen. <- Is my mother at the kitchen?

12. The teachers are in the classroom. -> Os professores estão na sala de aula. <-> The teachers are not in the classroom. <- Are the teachers in the classroom?

13. He is at home now. -> Ele está em casa agora. <-> He isn't at home now. <- Is he at home now?

14. He is a good student -> Ele é um bom aluno. <-> He is not a good student. <- Is he a good student?

15. It is a beautiful day. -> Hoje está um dia lindo. <-> It isn't a beautiful day. <- Is it a beautiful day?

16. My friends and I are excited. -> Meus amigos e eu estamos animados. <-> My friends and I are not excited. <- Are my friends and I excited?

17. The book is on the table. -> O livro está na mesa. <-> The book isn't on the table. <- Is the book on the table?

18. I am hungry. -> Eu estou com fome. <-> I am not hungry. <- Am I hungry?

19. You are very kind. -> Você é muito gentil. <-> You are not very kind. <- Are you very kind?

20. My parents are at work. -> Meus pais estão no trabalho. <-> My parents aren't at work. <- Are my parents at work?
