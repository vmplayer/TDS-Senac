## Tabela Verbo To Be
| Sujeito | Verbo | Contração |
| --- | --- | --- |
| I | am | I'm |
| You | are | You're |
| He | is | He's |
| She | is | She's |
| It | is | It's |
| We | are | We're |
| They | are | They're |

### Exercício:
Fazer duas frases com o verbo to be para cada sujeito

### I (Eu)
- **I** am just a man, miserable and fool, anyway. -> **I'm** just a man, miserable and fool, anyway.
- **I** lay stuck inside my bed, 'til the day comes over.

### You (Você/Vocês)
- **You** said You were my friend.
- **You** all had saw me for so long, so inoccent, like you all really are.

### He (Ele)
- **He** said to me make that indulgent recipe, so, he asked something to you too?
- **He** didn't asked nothing to you? So, what you'd do with this knife?

### She (Ela)
- **She** begun her escape before her death. Do you know something 'bout it?
- **She** doesn't got the right of run. Maybe this is our destiny, by the way.

### It (Isso)
- **It** was found on his coffin, about six days after his funeral.
- So, can I ask, what is the meaning of **it** to you?

### We (Nós)
- It's just our life, **we** are people that lives on abusive circunstances, that's all because this war never gets over.
- **We** are here to say, stop it. Stop it. Stop it. Stop it. Choke with your lies and meet the cold death, just disappear. -> **We're** here to say, stop it. Stop it. Stop it. Stop it. Choke with your lies and meet the cold death, just disappear.
